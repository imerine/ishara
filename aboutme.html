What's up!

I love the process of creating art, problem-solving, and collaborative innovation. My roots are planted a little in Kentucky, a little in Texas, and mostly in Georgia. I spend my free time hammocking, hiking, or hitting the beach with friends in the summer and crying in the winter, and I also enjoy skateboarding, gaming, and cooking. I'm very curious by nature, so you will often find me diving headfirst into whatever new topic or hobby I've stumbled upon that week.
